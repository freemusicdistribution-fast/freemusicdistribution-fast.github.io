FAQ

Free Music Distribution — Questions, Answered

Is Darkrooms Music Group really a free music distributor?

Yes. Darkrooms Music Group is a 100% free music distributor with no upfront fees. We operate on a small royalty-share model, so we only succeed when you succeed. No yearly fees like DistroKid pricing models or per-release fees.

How to distribute music for free?

It's incredibly simple. Create an account on our portal, upload your WAV/MP3 files, cover art, and song metadata. We handle everything else, pushing your tracks to global platforms without charging you a cent upfront.

How to get music on Spotify free?

When you release through Darkrooms Music Group, we automatically distribute your music to Spotify. As an official DSP partner, we get your music on Spotify, set up your Spotify for Artists profile, and help with pre-saves entirely for free.

What is the fastest music distributor?

We pride ourselves on offering fast music distribution free of charge. Most single, EP, and album distributions go live on all 150+ platforms within 72 hours of approval. For the best playlist results, however, we recommend submitting 3–4 weeks early.

Is Darkrooms an alternative to TuneCore or DistroKid?

Yes. We are considered one of the best free DistroKid alternatives. We offer VEVO distribution, post-release editing, playlist pitching, and priority support—features that often cost extra on other platforms.

Do you offer free music video distribution?

Yes. We provide free VEVO channel setup and official VEVO music video distribution, as well as syncing Official Artist Channels (OAC) on YouTube.

Do I keep my rights and royalties?

Absolutely. You retain 100% ownership of your masters. We are an independent music distributor, not a record label that takes ownership of your hard work.

Can I edit my release after it's live?

Yes. You can edit metadata, cover art, and even replace audio after release via our release management tools—a feature most free distributors don't offer.

Is this unlimited music distribution free?

Yes. Upload unlimited songs free. Whether it's a single, EP, or mixtape distribution, there are no limits on the amount of music you can release.
